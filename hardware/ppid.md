(Dell) Piece Part Identification (PPID)
=======================================

Scheme:

```
AA-BBBBBB-CCCCC-XYZ-EEEE-FFF
```

Where:

- AA: country of manufacture (not assembly)
- BBBBBB: part number
- CCCCC: ?
- XYZ: date (X: last digit of the year; Y: month [1-C], Z: day [1-V])
- EEEE: ?
- FFF: ?
